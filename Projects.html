Projects

Application name: NYCFTAeTix

This application will allow users in the New York City area to purchase bus and train tickets on their android devices. New York City currently does not have an application that will allow people to purchase transit tickets, despite New York state having similar applications for buses and trains leaving and arriving at port authority, penn station and other major transportation hubs. This application focus is to replace the need for a physical metro card.

Application name: Trivia Minute

Trivia Minute is a trivia game that allows users to answer a set of general trivia questions. Users can choose between four different difficulty levels (Easy, Medium, Hard, and Difficult), with each level having a total of 30 questions. Users have to beat the one-minute clock and see if they can answer all questions correctly.
